Learning Rate Schedule

lr = d_model-0.5 · min(step-0.5, step · warmup_steps-1.5)
Why warmup? Adam's running averages (moments) start at zero. In early steps, gradient estimates are noisy and biased. A low learning rate during warmup lets the moments accumulate meaningful statistics before the model takes larger steps. After warmup, the learning rate decays as 1/√step — gradual cooling as the model converges.
